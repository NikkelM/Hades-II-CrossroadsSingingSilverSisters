- Initial release.
- Adds lyrical versions of the songs Artemis, Melinoë and Apollo sing to the Music Maker in the Crossroads: "Moonlight Guide Us" (Artemis, Melinoë and duet), "Fated Glory" (Apollo), and "Time Belongs To Us" (the credits theme and its Crossroads arrangement).
- After unlocking the instrumental version of a song, you can now also unlock its lyrical version through the Music Maker in the Crossroads.
